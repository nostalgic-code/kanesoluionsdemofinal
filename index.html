A little about Kane Solutions

We are a solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Construction). We sell and install solar solutions for homes and businesses. Our company was incepted in 2011 as Kane Solutions. We incorporated our services company, For Communities Pty Ltd in 2020 to serve the communities that we live in. We trade as Kane Solutions.

Why Go Solar?

Lower Energy Bills

Save up to 70% on your electricity costs.

Solar panels significantly reduce your electricity bills by generating free energy from the sun. Over time, this leads to substantial savings and protection from rising utility costs.

Energy Independence

Reduce reliance on unreliable grid power.

With solar, you generate your own power, making you less dependent on load-shedding, power outages, or increasing electricity tariffs. It gives you control and peace of mind.

Eco-Friendly

Decrease your carbon footprint and support a cleaner Earth.

Solar energy is clean and renewable. By switching, you're helping reduce harmful greenhouse gas emissions and contributing to a more sustainable, healthier planet.
